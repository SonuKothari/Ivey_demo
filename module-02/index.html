What is a Chatbot?

A chatbot is a computer program that simulates a conversation with a human user. It can respond to text inputs (and sometimes voice), helping users get answers, perform tasks, or resolve issues without needing a live person.

In retail, chatbots are used for things like:

  • Answering FAQs about products or policies
  • Helping customers find the right size or color
  • Guiding users through returns or exchanges
  • Providing 24/7 support without needing staff online

What is ChatGPT?

ChatGPT, developed by OpenAI, is a powerful conversational AI that can:

  • Understand natural language
  • Respond to prompts with relevant, well-formed answers
  • Learn based on your instructions or uploaded data (like FAQs)

Unlike traditional chatbots with fixed scripts, ChatGPT is flexible. It can:

  • Handle unexpected questions
  • Provide human-like responses
  • Be trained quickly using a list of your most common customer questions

Why Use ChatGPT for Retail?

  • Reducing wait times
  • Giving consistent answers
  • Freeing up staff to handle complex or emotional concerns
  • Working around the clock (24/7 support!)
  • Adapting to changes quickly (update FAQs, no code needed)

You don’t need to know how to code—just upload your questions and answers, and ChatGPT will handle the rest!
