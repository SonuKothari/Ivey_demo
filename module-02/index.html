About this Module

In this module, you’ll roll up your sleeves and begin creating your own chatbot using the Jotform Chatbot Builder. This hands-on experience will show you how to structure real FAQ conversations that reflect your store’s customer needs, all without writing a single line of code.

Whether you choose a blank template or start from scratch, you’ll learn how to add questions, customize responses, and preview your chatbot in real time.

By the end of this module, you will be able to:

  • Navigate the Jotform chatbot interface with confidence.
  • Understand your Assignment 1.
  • Customize a chatbot with your own retail FAQs.
  • Preview, refine a working chatbot prototype.
