Module 2: Build Your Bot with Jotform

Now that you understand what chatbots can do, it's time to bring yours to life using Jotform’s no-code builder. You’ll build a chatbot that responds to real FAQs using drag-and-drop tools—fast, friendly, and functional.

Key Concepts

Step 1: Log In
Create a free Jotform account or sign in at jotform.com to access the chatbot builder.
Step 2: Start from Template
Choose “Create Chatbot” and start with a blank form or template designed for FAQs.
Step 3: Add Questions
Add your store’s top FAQs using the multiple-choice, paragraph, and short-text fields.
Step 4: Preview & Share
Test the bot inside Jotform and generate a shareable link or embed code for your site.

Task for This Module

  • Log in to Jotform.com
  • Start a chatbot project and enter at least 3 FAQ questions your store gets most often
  • Preview your bot in the browser

Once finished, take a screenshot of your chatbot preview—you’ll be asked to share it in the final module.
