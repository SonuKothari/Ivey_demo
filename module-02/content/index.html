What is Jotform?

Jotform is an intuitive platform that allows you to build online forms, surveys, and chatbots—no coding required. You can use it to easily create a retail-ready chatbot that answers common questions and automates basic support tasks.

  • Create and edit bots in a visual, form-based editor
  • Enable chatbot mode for conversational customer support
  • Apply conditional logic to control the flow
  • Share your chatbot using a link or embed it into your website

Steps to Create Chatbot

Step 1: Log In

Visit jotform.com and sign in or create a free account to begin building.

Step 2: Create Your Chatbot

Click “Create Form”, choose a blank template, then turn on chatbot mode in Settings.

Step 3: Add FAQs

Add key questions your customers ask using short text, dropdowns, or multiple-choice fields.

Step 4: Customize Logic

Use conditional logic to guide how the chatbot responds based on customer selections.

Step 5: Save & Prepare

Save your chatbot and get ready to polish, personalize, and test it in the next module.
