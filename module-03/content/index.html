Refine & Personalize Your Chatbot

Now that your chatbot is built, it’s time to refine how it sounds, flows, and represents your brand. In this module, you’ll clean up responses, adjust conversation structure, and apply personality through tone, visuals, and greetings.

These updates can turn a functional chatbot into a delightful assistant that supports your customers and reflects your store's voice.

Chatbot Refinement Flowchart

This flowchart shows how refinement decisions are made, from editing content to testing and finalizing your bot.

Steps to Refine & Personalize

Step 1: Edit Text

Review every response and rewrite where needed for tone, clarity, and friendliness.

Step 2: Personalize Greetings and Colors

Add or customize the welcome and goodbye messages to reflect your brand’s voice. Customize the colors to match your brand.

Step 3: Group Questions

Reorder or nest questions to improve logical flow and reduce repetition.

Step 4: Add Branding

Adjust bot name, avatar, colors, and fonts—where supported—to match your store’s design.

Step 5: Test & Iterate

Test the full conversation experience on both desktop and mobile, making notes to improve.

Step 6: Embed the Code

Once done, you will see the code that you can embed in your website.
