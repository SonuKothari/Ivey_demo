Welcome to Your Course!

In this first module, we’ll set the foundation by exploring what a chatbot actually is and why it’s quickly becoming an essential tool for modern retail. You’ll learn how FAQ chatbots can save your team time, reduce repetitive inquiries, and enhance your customer experience—all with minimal effort.

By the end of this course, you will be able to

  • Define what a chatbot is and describe its function in a retail setting.
  • Identify key benefits of using an FAQ chatbot for customer support.
  • Recognize common customer service challenges that chatbots can solve.
