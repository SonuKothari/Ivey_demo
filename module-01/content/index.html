What is a Chatbot?

A chatbot is a software tool designed to simulate conversation with users—typically via a website or messaging app. It uses programmed logic or AI to answer questions automatically, 24/7.

Examples

Click the arrows to explore real examples of how ChatGPT can respond to common retail customer questions.

Example 1

Customer: What sizes do you carry?
Bot: We offer S, M, L, XL, and XXL in most styles!

Example 2

Customer: How do I return a product?
Bot: You can return any item within 30 days by clicking “Return” on your order page.

Example 3

Customer: Is this shirt 100% cotton?
Bot: Yes! It’s made with 100% organic cotton.

Why Chatbots Matter in Retail?

Retail businesses face tons of repeat questions—returns, sizing, shipping, availability. Chatbots:

  • Instantly answer FAQs - improving response speed.
  • Reduce pressure on your staff.
  • Boost customer satisfaction by being available anytime.
  • Help capture leads or guide buyers toward a purchase.

Did you know?

Businesses using chatbots in ecommerce see up to 80% reduction in repetitive support requests.
