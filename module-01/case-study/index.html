Stride Supply Chatbot Case Study

Estimated reading time: 2–3 minutes

Overview

Stride Supply is a mid-sized footwear brand operating online and across retail outlets. As their customer base expanded, the team saw a spike in routine questions about stock availability, sizing guides, and shipping policies.

Challenge

During new product launches and seasonal sales, the customer service inbox overflowed with repeat questions—especially outside working hours. This led to slower response times and missed conversion opportunities.

Solution

The team implemented a customized chatbot using Jotform’s no-code builder. It was embedded directly on their product pages and homepage for maximum visibility.

  • Automated sizing support and store location lookups
  • Conditional logic for handling exchanges and delivery questions
  • Custom welcome message with brand-friendly tone

Results

  • 50% reduction in cart abandonment due to faster answers
  • Instant handling of off-hours inquiries with no added staff
  • Positive feedback from customers via post-chat surveys

"We were surprised how many customers relied on the bot to choose the right shoe size. It’s now part of the customer experience—and we built it in a weekend, no developers needed!"
— Operations Manager, Stride Supply

Takeaway

This case shows how a retail brand used no-code tools to increase efficiency and elevate their service without scaling their support team. Students can easily replicate this model by identifying their top customer questions and streamlining answers via chatbot logic.
