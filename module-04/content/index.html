Assignment: Design a Retail FAQ Chatbot

AI-Powered Assistant for a T-Shirt Retailer

Scenario

A t-shirt retailer has hired you to improve customer experience by building an AI-powered chatbot that lives on their website. This chatbot will answer frequently asked questions (FAQs) from online shoppers.

FAQs to Include

  • Available sizes and fit information.
  • Fabric types and care instructions.
  • Product availability and restocking.
  • Return and exchange policy.

Your Task

Build a simple FAQ chatbot using a no-code or low-code AI tool (e.g., Jotform, Tidio, Chatbase). The chatbot should be functional, user-friendly, and clearly designed to respond to common customer questions listed above.

Submission

Capture a screenshot or screen recording of your chatbot in action and upload it to the Chatbot Assignment Dropbox present in module 4. Make sure the chatbot responds accurately to at least three of the listed FAQs.

Tips for Success

  • Keep responses brief, clear, and customer-friendly.
  • Use the video walkthrough and treat modules as step by step guide to complete this assignment.
  • Use buttons or guided prompts where possible.
  • Test every pathway to ensure the bot works smoothly.
  • Customize the look and feel to match a retail vibe.
