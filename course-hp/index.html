Welcome to Your Course!

This course empowers retail leaders to build a fully functional FAQ chatbot using Jotform—no coding or technical expertise required. Through a step-by-step, hands-on approach, you’ll design and deploy a chatbot that enhances customer service by automating responses to common questions such as sizing, returns, and availability. By the end of the course, you'll have a ready-to-use chatbot that you can confidently share with customers to streamline support and save time.

What You’ll Do:

By the end of this course, you will be able to:

  • Learn what a chatbot is and why it matters in retail.
  • Build your own FAQ chatbot using Jotform.
  • Customize it with your store’s real FAQs.
  • Test and share your chatbot with customers.

Tools You’ll Use:

  • JotForm – To build and test your chatbot.
  • Web browser – Chrome, Microsoft Edge, or any modern web browser of your choice.

Course Requirements:

  • Time: 30 minutes of focused effort.
  • Device: Desktop or laptop recommended for the best building experience.
  • Internet: Stable connection.
  • Mindset: Curiosity + creativity (we’ll keep it fun!).

Show What You Built!

At the end of the course, you’ll upload a screenshot or short recording of your chatbot in action. It’s a great way to celebrate and reflect on what you’ve created!
